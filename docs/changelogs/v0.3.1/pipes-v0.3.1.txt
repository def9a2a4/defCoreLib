pipes v0.3.1

FLUID TRANSPORT (NEW)
- Pipes can now carry fluids pushed by DefCoreLib's Copper Water Pump:
  one bucket-unit per pump cycle travels the chain (corners included)
  to the first accepting tank, cauldron, or world endpoint.
- Every pipe variant carries water; only iron pipes carry lava, end to
  end. Item transport is unaffected.
- A dead-ended chain stalls the pump instead of spilling fluid.

LEGACY PIPE MIGRATION
- Pipes placed with the old standalone plugin (v0.2.0 and earlier),
  which stopped working under v0.3.0, are now automatically adopted
  into the current format as their chunks load; /pipes migrate sweeps
  all loaded chunks on demand (unloaded chunks migrate automatically
  as they load). Verified on a real world: 176 pipes across all
  8 variants migrated with zero leftover display entities.
- Stray display entities from broken legacy pipes are cleaned up, and
  /defcorelib cleanorphans now reports legacy strays it could not yet
  judge (unloaded chunks) as skipped instead of ignoring them.
- The migration shim will be removed in v0.4.0.
