mech v0.3.1

NEW BLOCK: THROTTLE LEVER
- A hand-set analog redstone source. Right-click to step the output
  up by 1, sneak-right-click to step it down (0-15); it powers
  adjacent redstone directly at that level (weighted-plate rules --
  run dust or lamps against it).
- Not a pressure plate: stepping on it never changes the signal, and
  the vanilla step and click are suppressed. Floor placement only.
- The spruce-fence handle tilts to show the current strength; the
  setting survives breaks, reloads, and restarts.
- Craft: a lever over an iron weighted pressure plate over a redstone
  block.

FLUID MACHINES (NEW)
- Copper Water Pump: floor-mounted machine that moves one bucket per
  cycle from below to a tank, cauldron, or pipe chain above. Wrench to
  reverse. A blocked route stalls the pump; fluid is never destroyed.
  Water only -- lava pumping is reserved for a future iron-tier pump.
- Steam stack: burner -> boiler -> steam piston, a new fuel-driven
  power source supplying 20 power. An empty-hand click reads out the
  stack status and names whatever is missing (fuel, water, boiler).
- Sieve now requires water: fill its tank by pipe or bucket; each batch
  of pans drinks one unit. A dry sieve idles with a dust cue.

NEW MACHINE: SIEVE
- Powered panning machine, wall-mounted on a container like the
  millstone and press. Washes gravel, sand, red sand, dirt, and soul
  sand into flint, nuggets, and rare finds (gems included, at low odds).

MACHINE POWER TIERS AND BALANCE
- Machines now draw power by work value (fan/placer 2, suction hopper
  3, press/pump 4, drill/millstone 5, sieve 10) instead of a flat 1, so
  a starter windmill no longer runs everything. The engine supplies 10.
- Piston cores and chain hoists still draw 1 but move at half speed.
- Sieve loot chances reduced across the board; sand's rare drop is now
  quartz instead of raw gold (red sand still pans raw gold).
- Fixed: chance-based recipes (sieve, millstone glass->sand) no longer
  consume input while the output container below is full -- the machine
  stalls like the mill and press do.

RECIPES
- New craft recipes for the sieve, Copper Water Pump, burner, boiler,
  and steam piston.
- mech now soft-depends on Pipes: with Pipes installed the pump recipe
  uses a copper pipe as the valve body; without it the pump recipe is
  simply not registered.

ADVANCEMENTS
- 16 new advancement nodes: "use" milestones for the drill, fan,
  placer, suction hopper, chain hoist, dynamo, clutch, and reverser,
  plus an operator capstone required by Grand Engineer; ore branches
  to precious/netherite for the millstone; panning treasure for the
  sieve; and a Pan Handler craft advancement for the new sieve.
- Fixed: machine products delivered straight into a container below now
  count toward product advancements, and items merely passing through a
  suction hopper no longer grant them.
- "Bound Together" now fires when you brush glue on, instead of on the
  first assembly.

OTHER
- Water wheel and boiler now sit on sealed copper-chest tile entities;
  adjacent custom chest blocks no longer render merged/offset. Water
  wheels placed before v0.3.1 keep working unchanged.
- The pump and boiler now face the player who places them, and the
  pump body stretches up to meet a horizontal corner pipe above.
- Visual polish: pump intake and valve art, boiler flame placement,
  larger steam piston head, unified machine smoke particles.
