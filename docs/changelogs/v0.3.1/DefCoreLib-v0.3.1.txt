DefCoreLib v0.3.1

Fluid handling arrives: a core fluid framework, the Copper Water Pump,
fluid-carrying pipes, a water-gated sieve, and a burner -> boiler ->
steam piston power stack. Also: tiered machine power, banners that
survive riding mechanisms, and automatic migration of legacy pipes.

FLUID HANDLING (NEW)
- New core fluid framework: whole-bucket water/lava units, an endpoint
  registry (machine tank > cauldron > world source), per-machine tanks,
  and a routing seam that the Pipes plugin plugs into.
- Copper Water Pump: floor-mounted machine that moves one bucket per
  cycle from below to a tank, cauldron, or pipe chain above. Wrench to
  reverse. A blocked route stalls the pump; fluid is never destroyed.
  Water only -- lava pumping is reserved for a future iron-tier pump.
- Steam stack: burner -> boiler -> steam piston, a new fuel-driven
  power source supplying 20 power. An empty-hand click reads out the
  stack status and names whatever is missing (fuel, water, boiler).
- Pipes carry fluids (pipes plugin): one bucket-unit per pump cycle
  travels the chain, corners included, to the first accepting endpoint.
  Every variant carries water; only iron pipes carry lava end to end.
  A dead-ended chain stalls the pump instead of spilling.
- Sieve now requires water: fill its tank by pipe or bucket; each batch
  of pans drinks one unit. A dry sieve idles with a dust cue.

NEW MACHINE: SIEVE
- Powered panning machine, wall-mounted on a container like the
  millstone and press. Washes gravel, sand, red sand, dirt, and soul
  sand into flint, nuggets, and rare finds (gems included, at low odds).

MACHINE POWER TIERS AND BALANCE
- Machines now draw power by work value (fan/placer 2, suction hopper
  3, press/pump 4, drill/millstone 5, sieve 10) instead of a flat 1, so
  a starter windmill no longer runs everything. The engine supplies 10.
- Piston cores and chain hoists still draw 1 but move at half speed.
- Sieve loot chances reduced across the board; sand's rare drop is now
  quartz instead of raw gold (red sand still pans raw gold).
- Fixed: chance-based recipes (sieve, millstone glass->sand) no longer
  consume input while the output container below is full -- the machine
  stalls like the mill and press do.

BANNERS RIDE MECHANISMS
- BetterBanners displays and vanilla banners (patterns intact) on a
  moving mechanism are carried in transit and re-attached on landing.
  If the landing spot can't host the banner, it drops as an item --
  banners are never silently destroyed. Banners on rope links that a
  chain hoist reels in drop as items too.
- Fixed a crash when a moved wall banner had to drop as an item.

LEGACY PIPE MIGRATION (pipes plugin)
- Pipes placed with the old standalone plugin (v0.2.0 and earlier),
  which stopped working under v0.3.0, are now automatically adopted
  into the current format as their chunks load; /pipes migrate sweeps
  all loaded chunks on demand. Stray displays from broken legacy
  pipes are cleaned up. The migration shim will be removed in v0.4.0.

RECIPES
- New craft recipes for the sieve, Copper Water Pump, burner, boiler,
  and steam piston. The mech plugin soft-depends on Pipes: with Pipes
  installed the pump recipe uses a copper pipe as the valve body;
  without it the pump recipe is simply not registered.

ADVANCEMENTS (mech plugin)
- 16 new advancement nodes: "use" milestones for eight machines and
  components, an operator capstone required by Grand Engineer, ore
  branches to precious/netherite, panning treasure, and Pan Handler
  for crafting the sieve.
- Fixed: products delivered straight into a container below now count
  toward product advancements; suction-hopper pass-through no longer
  false-grants; "Bound Together" fires on brushing glue, not the first
  assembly.

OTHER
- Water wheel and boiler now sit on sealed copper-chest tile entities;
  adjacent custom chest blocks no longer render merged/offset. Water
  wheels placed before v0.3.1 keep working unchanged.
- The pump and boiler now face the player who places them, and the
  pump body stretches up to meet a horizontal corner pipe above.
- Visual polish: pump intake and valve art, boiler flame placement,
  larger steam piston head, unified machine smoke particles.
- New events for companion plugins: GlueAppliedEvent,
  MachineActedEvent, MachineProducedEvent.
- Docs: fluids design doc; the 3D block viewer renders water and lava
  as tinted translucent cubes; showcases updated for the power tiers.
