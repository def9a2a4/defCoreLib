mech v0.3.2

NEW BLOCK: THROTTLE LEVER
- A hand-set analog redstone source. Right-click to step the output
  up by 1, sneak-right-click to step it down (0-15); it powers
  adjacent redstone directly at that level (weighted-plate rules --
  run dust or lamps against it).
- Not a pressure plate: stepping on it never changes the signal, and
  the vanilla step and click are suppressed. Floor placement only.
- The spruce-fence handle tilts to show the current strength; the
  setting survives breaks, reloads, and restarts.
- Craft: a stick over a row of redstone dust, iron ingot, and
  cobblestone, over three smooth stone slabs.

NEW MACHINE: MECHANICAL DISPENSER
- A vanilla dispenser that becomes a launcher (higher speed, increased accuracy/consistency) when fed rotation
  power; unpowered it stays an ordinary dispenser (vanilla GUI,
  hoppers, and item pipes all keep working). Glows with red eyes
  while active.
- Craft: a slime block above a piston flanked by two bearings, above
  a dispenser flanked by redstone.

RECIPES
- Burner recipe reworked -- now shapeless: a blast furnace, a dropper,
  and any copper grate. The old copper/blast-furnace/iron recipe is
  retired.
- Drill recipe no longer needs netherite -- now a diamond over an
  iron-flanked piston over a full row of iron.

RENAMED
- The "Mechanism" family is now "Mechanical": the Mechanism Minecart
  becomes the Mechanical Minecart (now a lodestone-headed item);
  advancement text renames the Extendable Piston to Mechanical Piston.

ADVANCEMENTS
- New nodes: craft/use milestones for the throttle lever and
  mechanical dispenser; craft milestones for the Copper Water Pump
  and the burner -> boiler -> steam piston stack; and a steam-power
  network milestone.
- Tree tidy-up: the chain pulley now hangs under the gear, and the
  chain hoist has its own icon.
