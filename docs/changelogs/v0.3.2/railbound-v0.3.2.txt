railbound v0.3.2

Initial release

TRAINS
- Couple minecarts into a train by right-clicking them with a chain
  (shears to uncouple). Any fuelled locomotive anywhere in the train
  drives the whole thing along the rails, and top speed doesn't drop
  as the train gets longer. A cosmetic chain renders between coupled
  carts (chain-visuals toggle).
- Couplings snap if two carts are pulled too far apart or the rail is
  mined out from under a moving train; the chain drops as an item.

CARTS
- Blast-furnace cart: the engine -- three fuel slots plus in-GUI -/+
  buttons that set a cruise speed in redstone-sized steps, and a high,
  configurable top speed. Emits dynamic light while lit (optional
  DynLight plugin). A plain lit furnace minecart also works, weaker.
- Coal tender: a 9-slot fuel-only inventory that auto-feeds any
  furnace or blast-furnace cart in the same train.
- Dispenser cart: fires one item straight up over a powered activator
  rail; projectile items launch as their real projectiles.
- Hoppers and containers load carts: a hopper pointing in, or a
  container directly above, fills a cart -- fuel carts take only fuel,
  the dispenser cart takes anything.

RAILS
- Special rails must sit on flat, straight track -- placed on a slope
  or curve they pop back off as an item.
- Junction: a 4-way crossing -- carts keep their heading instead of
  curving, so lines can cross without turning.
- Controller: sets a train's cruise speed from its redstone signal
  (0 stop, 15 full) and holds it after the train leaves; a run of
  unpowered controllers makes a stop-and-hold station.
- Destructor: drops a passing cart and its whole contents into the
  container below, or on the ground. Mechanism (glue) minecarts are
  exempt; vanilla chest/furnace/TNT carts are recycled too.

REQUIRES
- DefCoreLib: the carts and rails live there; this plugin enables
  their recipes and drives the logic. Tuning in carts-config.yml.
