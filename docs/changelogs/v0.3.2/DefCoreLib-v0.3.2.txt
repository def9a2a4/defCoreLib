DefCoreLib v0.3.2

Hosts the new Railbound content and adds an optional
dynamic-light hook. (https://modrinth.com/plugin/dynlight)

- Ships the Railbound cart and rail blocks and drives the
  train, fuel, coupling, and special-rail logic when the
  Railbound companion is installed (recipes gated by it;
  without it the blocks exist via /defcorelib give but aren't
  craftable and trains don't drive).
- Optional DynLight integration: a lit blast-furnace cart emits
  dynamic light via the DynLight plugin (no hard dependency; inert
  when absent). Moving mechanisms now emit their light at the block
  rather than the pivot.
